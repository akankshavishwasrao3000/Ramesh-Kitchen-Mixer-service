Cancellation & Refund Policy

Last Updated: {% now "F j, Y" %}

We want to make sure you are happy with your purchase and repair services from Ramesh Kitchen Mixer. Here is how we handle cancellations and refunds.

1. Order Cancellation

You can cancel your orders or repair requests before they are processed or before a technician is sent out. If we have already dispatched the item or started the repair, please contact us to discuss the next steps.

2. Refund Verification

Refunds are processed after a quick verification of the product or issue. We may ask you to send us a photo or bring the item to our shop so we can take a look at the problem before approving the refund.

3. Payment Processing Time

Once your refund is verified and approved, it will be processed back to your original payment method via Razorpay. Please note that it can take 5 to 7 business days for the money to appear in your bank account.

4. Repair Service Refunds

For repair services, refunds depend on whether we were able to fix the issue or if the required spare parts were available. If we cannot repair your mixer, any advance service fees paid will be appropriately refunded.

Need to request a cancellation or refund?

Please call us directly or use our contact page.
